PART II: POWERS AND FUNCTIONS OF THE COMMISSION
- The Commission has the power to issue directives and notices to individuals, service providers, and internet intermediaries who publish or communicate prohibited or false statements. 🚫📝
- The Commission can refer matters to the court, especially those that may be in contempt of court or potentially harm the judiciary's authority and impartiality. ⚖️👩‍⚖️
- The Commission has the authority to maintain an online portal to inform the public about false statements, specify declared online locations, and recommend disabling access to certain information. 💻🌐
- The Commission can issue codes of practice for service providers and internet intermediaries, register websites providing social media platforms, and consult with those who may be affected by its actions. 📋🤝
- The Commission can advise the government on online safety matters, acquire and dispose of property, enter contracts, obtain police assistance, and generally perform any acts necessary to exercise its powers and functions. 🏛️🔒 #OnlineSafety #CommissionPowers @OnlineSafetyCommission
